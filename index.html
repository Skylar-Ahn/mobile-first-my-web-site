Quality Signals

Dataset health panel

Live Model
schema PASS References and required fields are valid.
bbox REVIEW Possible out-of-bounds or abnormal aspect ratio.
boundary AMBIGUOUS Task transition point needs evidence check.
leakage CHECK Near-duplicate sample may cross splits.
low_conf REVIEW Route uncertain prediction to human queue.
taxonomy HIGH Label guideline mismatch affects downstream training.
class_tail CHECK Long-tail class needs distribution report review.

bbox REVIEW: likely cause is an invalid box, unusual size, or edge crop. Action: route sample to human review. Priority: medium-high.

boundary AMBIGUOUS: likely cause is an unclear manipulation transition. Action: compare wrist/front camera, 3D motion view, and joint-state signal.
